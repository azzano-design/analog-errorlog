About the studio

Azzano Design is an independent studio with a tight focus: software that helps artists do their best work. We believe the best tools are the ones you truly love using.

As artists, sometimes our experiments don't always work out... this site is a journal of sorts of various explorations, the goal of which is to help others discover new tools and ideas.
